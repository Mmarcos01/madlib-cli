Welcome to Python Madlibs!

A Mad Lib is an interactive story that uses words of your choice. 
When prompted, you will be asked to insert a specific type of word. 
Once you have added all words necessary the story will be created.

Instructions: 

When prompted type in your word then press enter to move on to the next word. 
Once all words have been collected, your story will print.

> Press Enter to Begin
